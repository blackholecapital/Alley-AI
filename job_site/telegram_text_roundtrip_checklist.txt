  curl -s "https://api.telegram.org/bot${TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN}/setWebhook" \

  curl -s "https://api.telegram.org/bot${TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN}/getWebhookInfo"
  Expect: url matches step 1; pending_update_count == 0;
          last_error_message empty/absent.

  Action: in Telegram client, send "hello" to the bot.
  Expect: Worker receives POST /telegram/webhook with a valid
          X-Telegram-Bot-Api-Secret-Token; Worker responds 200;
          operator sees bot reply in Telegram client within a few seconds.
